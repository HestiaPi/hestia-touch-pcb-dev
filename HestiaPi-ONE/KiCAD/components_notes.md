# General Notes

- The prices listed here do not include tax nor shipping
- The prices are a snapshot in time. The prices will change over time
- Use alternative components at our own risk. They might work, they might not
- Approximate total cost of for one complete unit (3 relays, US power supply)

  - Tax (varies by jursidiction): $18
